Traveling salesman problem


There are different versions of TSP but there core problem stays the same. The problem is to find the shortest path or tour through all the given points or cities as they are often called. The traveling salesman problem is a proven NP-complete problem. My demos show the closed TSP variant, in 2d space, which requires that the last city in the tour is the same as the first city.

Many algorithms have been adapted and invented to solve different versions of the TSP. Here I showcase some popular ways to arrive at a good solutions to the problem even though none of them guarantee an optimal solution or even a good solution in fact. They are used because they are faster than the algorithms that guarantee the optimal solution for any give instance of the TSP.
